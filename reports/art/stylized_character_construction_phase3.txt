PHASE 3 - STYLIZED CHARACTER CONSTRUCTION

Summary
- The subject builder now has modular stylized body construction instead of relying only on generic blocky procedural bodies.
- Added archetype-specific body templates for archon, solar warrior and guide mage.
- Each new archetype build includes structured parts: head shape, torso mass, cloak or armor mass, dominant arm, support arm, lower body stance, weapon anchor point and symbol anchor point.
- The active subject routing now uses these archetype templates in both silhouette generation and detail generation.

- game/art/silhouette_builder.py

Implemented changes
- Added `_build_humanoid_parts(...)` as a reusable structured part builder.
- Added helper paint functions for stylized polygon-based anatomy:
  - `_poly(...)`
  - `_line(...)`
  - `_paint_parts(...)`
- Replaced primitive silhouette internals with modular silhouette generation for:
  - `generate_humanoid_silhouette()`
  - `generate_archon_silhouette()`
  - `generate_solar_warrior_silhouette()`
  - new `generate_guide_mage_silhouette()`
- Added archetype-specific detail builders:
  - `_draw_archon_character(...)`
  - `_draw_solar_warrior_character(...)`
  - `_draw_guide_mage_character(...)`
- Updated `draw_subject_silhouette(...)` to route mage subjects through the new guide mage silhouette.
- Updated `draw_subject(...)` so archon, warrior and mage families use the new stylized modular character construction instead of falling back to the older block-based variants.

What improved structurally
- Body mass is no longer just accidental overlap of circles and rectangles.
- The silhouette now has more directed shape language per archetype:
  - archon: vertical, ceremonial, throne-like and oppressive
  - solar warrior: triangular, forward-driving, weapon-led
  - guide mage: calm vertical robe mass with staff and symbol focus
- Weapon and symbol anchors are now part of subject construction instead of being implied later.

Warnings
- This phase improves character construction, but it does not yet map lore and tags deeply into pose logic beyond archetype-level routing.
- Some legacy functions still exist in the file for backward compatibility, but the main subject path now routes through the new modular builders.
- Final metric success is still expected to depend on Phase 4 and Phase 5, because tag-to-shape behavior and contrast policy are not fully upgraded yet.
