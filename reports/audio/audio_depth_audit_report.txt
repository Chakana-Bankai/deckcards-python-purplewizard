[monotony_sources]
1. context_specs are too few and rely on broad aliases that collapse identity.
2. reward and codex do not have their own routed music contexts.
3. credits reuses victory, flattening ending identity.
4. long bgm files carry useful loop closure around 24s, then keep redundant tail.
5. stingers are recognisable but still use small fixed phrases and limited families.
6. ui/gameplay sfx table is incomplete, so many actions share the same sonic gesture.
7. synthesis favours pad+bass+motif with low layer separation; percussion and ornament are underused.

[recommendation]
Introduce canonical context specs, a layered theme builder, context-specific stinger families, a complete UI/gameplay SFX registry, and explicit state routing for codex/credits/reward before wider soundtrack regeneration.
