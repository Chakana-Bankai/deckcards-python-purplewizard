immersion_music_specs_report

[summary]
Music specs were corrected to avoid a universal dark low-BPM fallback and to force clearer context separation.

[global_corrections]
1. Every context now has an explicit target BPM range.
2. Every context now has harmonic color, not only scale/mode.
3. Melody is mandatory in all canonical contexts.
4. Percussion is mandatory only where motion or tension requires it.
5. Stinger family existence is explicit per context.
6. The spec now prevents organ-like sustained-tone defaulting by requiring instrumentation palettes per role.
