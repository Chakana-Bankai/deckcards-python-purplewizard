summary
- Runtime beta foundation is already centered around combat, cards, lore, UI screens, and content services under `game/`.

- Art/audio/QA tooling is extensive and useful, but currently fragmented across canonical pipeline modules, wrappers, and historical execution passes.
- Repository contains clear consolidation opportunities without blind deletion.

cross_cutting_findings
- `tools/chakana_studio.py` is the best existing canonical CLI anchor.
- Runtime canonical center of gravity is `game/main.py` + `game/combat/*` + `game/cards/*` + `game/lore/*` + `game/services/*`.
- The repo already follows the future beta priorities conceptually, but the tooling surface is noisier than the runtime.
- `game/.venv/` exists inside the runtime tree and pollutes discovery/audit scans; this should not remain part of canonical engine layout.
- `game/map/` and `game/chakana_world/` are structurally underdefined compared with combat/cards/lore.
