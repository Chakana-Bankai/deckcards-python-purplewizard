# CHAKANA Art Direction Document

## Goal
Provide clear artistic direction for procedural visual systems while preserving readability and lore identity.

## Structural Rule (Every Generated Image)
Each generated image must include:
1. Subject
2. Action
3. Environment

## Composition Targets
- Subject: 50-60%
- Environment: 20-30%
- Energy/Action FX: 10-20%

## Visual Themes
- Gaia Mountains: sacred stone silhouettes, ritual fog, highland light gradients.
- Hyperborea Temples: advanced ancient architecture, crystal circuitry, marble/ice contrast.
- Archon Realm: corrupted geometry, oppressive void masses, crimson fractures.
- The Void: negative-space dominance, rune noise, cosmic fracture fields.

## Symbol Library
- Chakana
- Huemul
- Sacred geometry
- Void runes

## Set Style Mapping
- BASE: mystic geometry, Chakana motifs, gold/violet palette.
- HIPERBOREA: atlantean-polar temples, crystal technology, white/ice/ancient gold palette.
- ARCHON: dark entities, void corruption, gnostic horror, dark purple/crimson/black palette.

## Avatar Direction
- Portrait format.
- Focus on face.
- Chakana symbol visible.
- Golden energy accents (controlled, not blown out).

## Pipeline Notes
- No stretched art.
- Keep subject readability above overlay density.
- Legendary cards receive premium detail/glow pass.
- Avoid repetitive geometry by motif + seed diversity.
